Security

Bank-grade protection, built in

Your financial data deserves the same security as the banks themselves.

🔒 AES-256 Encryption

All data encrypted at rest on your device using Apple's NSFileProtectionComplete — the same standard used by financial institutions.

🔑 Secure Keychain

Authentication tokens stored in the iOS Keychain with device-only access. Your credentials never leave your phone.

🛡️ Plaid Security

Bank connections handled by Plaid — the same infrastructure trusted by Venmo, Robinhood, and thousands of financial apps.

📱 Local-First

Your financial data lives on your device, not our servers. We can't see your balances, transactions, or accounts.
